Erika Obonai

In 2000, I was born in Tokyo Prefecture. Living in Miyagi.

I study Society at Tohoky University. I'm interested in family caregiving and the role of women in the home.

After taking a statistics class, I discovered the joy of programming.

Self-taught in web development. I try to create simple web pages with the user's best interest in mind.It is created with an emphasis on unity and convenience.

In the future, I hope to work in the IT industry. I would like to be an existence where my creations can influence someone's sense of values.
